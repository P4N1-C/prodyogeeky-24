ABOUT US

Welcome to ProdyoGeeky, where innovation meets enthusiasm! We are the dynamic force behind NIT's premier technical extravaganza. ProdyoGeeky is not just an event; it's a celebration of technology, creativity, and passion. As the flagship techfest organized by our esteemed technical club, we strive to create an unparalleled platform for tech enthusiasts to showcase their brilliance. From riveting competitions and insightful workshops to inspiring talks by industry leaders, ProdyoGeeky is the ultimate hub for all things tech. Join us on this thrilling journey of exploration and discovery, where the future of innovation unfolds. Embrace the geek in you at ProdyoGeeky!

EVENTS

Get ready to dive into the tech universe at PRODYOGEEKY, our college's annual tech fest! Brace yourself for a thrilling fusion of innovation and excitement, featuring events like Cadathon, where designs come to life; FPV Drone Race, a high-flying spectacle; and a mind-bending Treasure Hunt that'll keep you on your toes. PRODYOGEEKY is not just an event; it's a celebration of technology, creativity, and camaraderie. Whether you're a coding maestro, a drone enthusiast, or someone eager to unravel mysteries, there's something for everyone. Join us for a day of cutting-edge competitions, engaging workshops, and an unforgettable tech experience. Embrace the geek in you - PRODYOGEEKY is where innovation takes center stage!
